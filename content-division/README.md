# BioKev Content Production Division

A content-production division bolted onto the Biotech Intelligence Desk. Its job: turn pharmacist-led biotech research into short-form video (LinkedIn, TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts) in a bright, hand-drawn, clinical-but-fun style — with clear disclosures and zero hype.

## Positioning
Kevin is not claiming to already be a famous biotech expert. He is building a public body of pharmacist-led biotech research and teaching people how to understand the science, catalysts, and risks behind biotech investing.

## Style
1. Kevin speaking directly to camera. 2. A recurring 2D animated avatar ("BioKev"). 3. Bright hand-drawn visuals. 4. Clinical-trial & FDA education. 5. Biotech investing analysis. 6. Clear disclosures, no hype.

## The seven agents (see `agents/`)
1. **Short-Form Content Strategist** — research → ranked short-form ideas + repeatable formats.
2. **Biotech Scriptwriter** — 30/45/60s scripts; PharmD credibility engine; no exaggerated claims.
3. **Avatar Creative Director** — owns the BioKev avatar + style bible + AI prompt library.
4. **Animation Storyboard Agent** — script → shot-by-shot plan; Kevin-vs-avatar; visual metaphors.
5. **Compliance & Credibility Editor** — the gate; fact/assumption/opinion separation; disclosures.
6. **Caption & Editing Agent** — captions, title cards, lower thirds; platform optimization.
7. **Analytics & Iteration Agent** — performance tracking; next experiments.

## The pipeline
```
Research report (Intelligence Desk)
   → [01] short-form ideas
   → [02] script (30/45/60s)
   → [05] compliance review  ← gate
   → [04] storyboard (Kevin vs avatar + metaphors)
   → VO (ElevenLabs) + animation (Character Animator/Hedra/Runway/Krea/Luma)
   → [06] captions + platform versions (CapCut)
   → schedule + post
   → [07] analytics → next week's ideas
```

## Deliverables (see `deliverables/`)
- `content-calendar-30-day.md` — 30-day short-form calendar.
- `scripts/` — 10 complete scripts (evergreen written in full; company-specific use verify-placeholders).
- `storyboards/` — 10 shot-by-shot storyboards.
- `visual-identity-guide.md` — brand/visual system.
- `avatar-design-brief.md` — recurring avatar style bible.
- `ai-prompt-library.md` — prompts for Hedra/Runway/Krea/Luma/Character Animator/Cartoon Animator.
- `production-workflow.md` — ElevenLabs → Hedra/Character Animator/Runway/Krea/Luma → CapCut.
- `disclaimer-disclosure-policy.md` — disclosure/compliance policy.
- `report-to-3-videos-system.md` — turn every company report into ≥3 short-form videos.

## Hard rules
No price targets, no "buy/sell", no invented dates or data. Estimated dates labeled estimated. Statistical ≠ clinical significance. Disclosures on every video. Educational, not advice.
