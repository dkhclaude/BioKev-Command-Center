# BIOKEV — Visual Identity Guide

**Owner:** Kevin (PharmD) · **Brand:** BioKev Biotech Intelligence
**Positioning line:** *A pharmacist building a public body of biotech research — teaching the science, catalysts, and risks behind biotech investing.*
**Version:** 1.0 · Maintained by the Avatar Creative Director agent

---

## 1. Brand in one sentence

BioKev is a pharmacist-led biotech research creator. The visual identity is **biotech lab meets Wall Street research desk**, rendered in bright, hand-drawn, street-art-influenced 2D animation. Smart and clinical, funny when it earns it, never clownish, never hype.

## 2. Positioning guardrails (visual + verbal)

- We are *building* credibility in public, not claiming to already be a famous expert.
- Every asset separates **fact / assumption / opinion** visually (see color coding below).
- No green-candle "to the moon" imagery, no rocket ships, no lambos, no fake price targets.
- Disclosures are part of the design system, not an afterthought.

## 3. Color palette

| Role | Name | HEX | Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary | Hot pink | `#FF4DB2` | Brand accent, avatar signature, key highlights, CTAs |
| Secondary | Biotech yellow | `#FFD12A` | Section headers, callouts, "insight" moments |
| Alert | Alert red | `#FF1E2D` | Risk, binary events, warnings, "bear case" |
| Base dark | Deep black | `#111111` | Backgrounds, text on light, lab-desk base |
| Base light | Off-white | `#F4F4F4` | Backgrounds, captions, negative space |
| Accent | Accent gray | `#2B2B2B` | Cards, dividers, secondary surfaces |

**Semantic color coding (used across video + dashboard):**
- **Fact / confirmed** → biotech yellow underline or chip
- **Assumption / estimate** → hot pink dashed outline
- **Opinion / thesis** → gray chip labeled "opinion"
- **Risk / binary** → alert red

Contrast rule: never place hot pink text on alert red, or yellow on off-white. Body text is `#111111` on `#F4F4F4`, or `#F4F4F4` on `#111111`.

## 4. Typography

- **Display / headers:** a bold condensed marker-style face (e.g., *Anton*, *Bebas Neue*, or a hand-drawn *Permanent Marker* for accents). ALL-CAPS for section headers.
- **Body / captions:** a clean geometric sans (e.g., *Inter*, *Montserrat*). High legibility at 9:16 mobile size.
- **Numbers / data:** a monospace or tabular-figure sans for tickers, dates, endpoints (e.g., *Roboto Mono*).
- Caption safe-area: keep text within the center 80% and above the bottom 15% (platform UI overlap).

## 5. Visual style rules

- Bright saturated fills, black hand-drawn outlines (2–4px, slightly wobbly / marker feel).
- Street-art / marker energy: halftone dots, tape, sketch arrows, hand-drawn underlines, "highlighter" swipes in yellow.
- Motion: snappy, slightly overshooting easing. Pop-in elements, not slow fades.
- Backgrounds: the recurring **command-center lab desk** — beakers, a whiteboard of trial timelines, a candlestick chart drawn in marker, sticky notes with catalyst dates.
- Never: stock-photo people, corporate blue gradients, glossy 3D, clip-art, AI-uncanny faces on the real Kevin.

## 6. Recurring visual metaphors (house style)

| Concept | Visual metaphor |
|---|---|
| Mechanism of action | A hand-drawn "lock and key" or a molecule high-fiving a receptor |
| FDA / PDUFA catalyst | A calendar page with a red stamp; a referee (FDA) holding a card |
| Trial endpoint hit/miss | A dart hitting / missing a bullseye; a finish-line ribbon |
| Statistical vs clinical significance | A tiny "p<0.05" trophy vs a patient actually feeling better |
| Cash runway | An airplane on a runway that's running out of tarmac |
| Dilution | A glass of juice getting watered down; pie chart slices shrinking |
| Risk / binary event | A coin mid-flip; a fork in the road with red signage |
| Bull vs bear case | A cartoon bull and bear at a lab bench, not a stock-chart cliché |

## 7. Logo & lockups

- **Wordmark:** "BIOKEV" in the marker display face, hot pink, with a small yellow highlighter swipe under "BIO".
- **Avatar mark:** the BioKev head (see avatar brief) inside a rounded-square badge, deep-black background.
- **Safe space:** clear space = height of the "K" on all sides.

## 8. Lower thirds & title cards (spec)

- **Lower third:** rounded black card, hot-pink left edge bar, white name "BioKev — PharmD", yellow role line "Biotech research, not advice".
- **Title card (open):** yellow marker headline on black, ticker chip top-right, "Not investment advice" micro-disclosure bottom.
- **End card:** avatar waving, hot-pink "Follow for the next readout" + platform handles.

## 9. Disclosure design tokens

Every video carries a persistent, legible disclosure. Design tokens:
- `disclosure/onscreen`: small off-white text, bottom safe area, present ≥3s: *"Educational only. Not investment or medical advice."*
- `disclosure/position`: when a ticker is discussed and a position exists, a yellow chip: *"Position: [long/none]"*.
- `disclosure/estimate`: any estimated date gets a pink dashed "EST." tag.

## 10. Do / Don't

**Do:** bright, hand-drawn, clinical clarity, one idea per shot, disclosures visible, humor that serves comprehension.
**Don't:** hype, fake certainty, price targets stated as facts, clickbait that the script can't back up, clownish mugging that undercuts the clinical credibility.
