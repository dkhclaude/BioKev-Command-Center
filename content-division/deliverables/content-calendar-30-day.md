# BioKev — 30-Day Short-Form Content Calendar

**Cadence:** 1 primary short/day, 5–6 days/week, with a weekly newsletter + 1 LinkedIn insight post. Each short is cut into platform versions (TikTok / Reels / Shorts) and, where it teaches an idea, a LinkedIn 1:1 version.
**Weekly rhythm (recurring "shows"):**
Mon *Catalyst in 60* · Tue *PharmD Explains* · Wed *Trial Design Teardown* · Thu *Cash/Dilution* · Fri *Bull vs Bear or Myth vs Mechanism* · Sat *Post-Mortem / recap* · Sun off / repurpose.

> Company-specific slots (Catalyst, Teardown, Bull/Bear, Post-Mortem) require a verified research file before filming. Evergreen slots ship anytime. `[VERIFY]` = confirm a real date/fact before publishing.
