**[BODY — Avatar takes over, 7–45s]**
"PDUFA stands for the Prescription Drug User Fee Act. In plain terms: the company pays a fee, and in exchange the FDA agrees to *decide* on their drug by a target date. That target is the PDUFA date. **[yellow chip: FACT]**

On that date, roughly three things can happen: approval, a Complete Response Letter — a 'not yet, fix these things' — or a delay. **[avatar red stamp gag]**

Here's the catch most people miss: the PDUFA date is a *decision deadline*, not a guaranteed approval. And the FDA can act early, or push it. **[avatar coin-flip]**"

**[TURN — Avatar, 45–52s]**
"So it's a binary event. Big upside, real downside, sometimes overnight."
